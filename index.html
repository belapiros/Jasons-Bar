aboutjasons

Jason's Bar and Kitchen is the brainchild of Ryan and John McElhinney.

We are located at 96 Isabella street ( behind Southwark Tube ) SE1 8HA.

The original Jason's was opened in Ireland in 1970 by their father Jason (now sunning himself in Spain) so the boys decided to pay tribure to their father by re-opening the original almost forty years on.
